Summer 2027 Research Pipeline

Summer-2027 research internships — a route to a recommendation letter and research experience, not income. The autumn-2026 sprint (Yessenov packet + direct outreach to researchers) sets these up. Keep them from eating into the real-analysis and coursework time.

The actual binding deadline is INTERNAL, not external. Phase 0 reproduction artifact must be "showable" (cold-emailable repo link with at least one empirical extension result) by Oct 15 2026. Miss this gate and cold-email reply rates collapse 15-25% → 1-3%, Yessenov host-invite signal disappears, and every fallback becomes a sub-apex placement that displaces OPERATIVE without yielding apex-quality letter. External deadlines are downstream of this internal gate.
