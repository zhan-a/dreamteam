The engine — why pre-load now (the flywheel)

Arrive at the MSc pre-loaded → the hard measure-theory core is a review → freed bandwidth → an early paid embed in a professor's funded project → a paper-shaped thesis → co-authorship + a warm PI intro + a Europe-wide network → reach the PhD gate with a surplus. MSc admission is rule-based (guaranteed on the floors), so the target is outstanding on arrival, not out-ranking anyone at a door.

The honest caveat

The salaried PhD is a reach — supervisor-gated, few seats, dependent on a specific PI recruiting in the right cycle. Pre-loading maximises the odds and the German floor de-risks it; neither makes it guaranteed. Effort is controllable; the outcome is not — which is exactly why the effort is maximal, and aimed only at the high-leverage levers (the artifact, domain depth, the PI relationship, grades, GRE-Q).
